Our mission is to provide the best white water rafting experience possible while promoting the conservation of our natural resources.

History

Our company was founded in 1995 by a group of friends who shared a passion for adventure and the outdoors. We started with just one raft and a dream to share the thrill of white water rafting with others. Over the years, we have grown into one of the leading rafting companies in the country, but our commitment to providing a safe and unforgettable experience remains the same.
